How To Make Lulu's Banana Bread

This is one of my all-time favorite recipes. I've been making it since I was a little girl. It is so simple yet so delicious!

What You'll Need

  • 3 large ripe bananas
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • dash of cinnamon
  • 1 egg
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt

What To Do

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Melt the 4 tbsp of butter and allow it to cool.
  3. Mash the bananas.
  4. Beat together the sugar, eggs, butter, vanilla, and mashed bananas using an electric mixer.
  5. Mix together the remaining dry ingredients and then add this to the banana mixture.
  6. Blend together for a couple of minutes.
  7. Bake for 45-50 minutes.
